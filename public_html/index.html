Welcome to Squaree Game
Who are we?
Our company is Squaree, we design simple, easy-to-pickup multiplayer games that are fun to play.

Our first game is bomberman. Your goal is to strategically place bombs in order to destroy obstables and kill other players in the game. You can also eat powerups to increase your speed, range, and number of bombs.
What do we sell?
In the catalogue section, you can buy in-game items to boost up your abilities in game.
